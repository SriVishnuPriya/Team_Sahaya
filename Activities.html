OUR MISSION

To provide care and assistance in both practical and emotional terms to everyone in the community

The Climate Crisis

The climate crisis enhances inequality, poverty, and displacement, as well as raising the risk of conflict. We raised funds up to $15000 to help the needy.

COVID-19 Pandemic

COVID-19 has robbed millions of children not only of their education, safety, and health but also of their childhood. Save the Children has been on the front lines of the pandemic since the beginning, thanks to the gratitude of our supporters.



Education

We acknowledge the importance of education for girls.
We will work together to improve girls' education. We will reach every girl in order to assist her to grow up healthy, educated, and safe.
